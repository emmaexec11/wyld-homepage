Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Naturally Wyld's natural skincare products.

Are your products really 100% natural?

Yes! All Naturally Wyld products are made with 100% natural ingredients, carefully selected based on EWG safety guidelines. We never use harsh chemicals, synthetic fragrances, parabens, or sulfates. Every ingredient serves a purpose and is safe for even the most sensitive skin.

What makes ozonated oils special?

Ozonated oils are medical-grade healing oils infused with ozone gas, creating powerful compounds that promote skin repair and regeneration. We're the only Canadian brand offering this proven technology, which is clinically shown to help with eczema, dry skin, cuts, and anti-aging.

How fast is shipping to Canada?

We ship from Kelowna, BC within 1-2 business days. Most Canadian orders arrive within 3-7 business days. Free shipping on orders over $50, and all Subscribe & Save orders include free shipping regardless of order size.

Why don't you use baking soda in your deodorants?

Baking soda can irritate sensitive skin and cause darkening of underarms over time. Our aluminum-free deodorants use gentle, effective alternatives that work all day without irritation - even for the most sensitive skin.

Is your packaging really plastic-free?

Yes! All our packaging is 100% compostable and plastic-free. We use sustainable materials like cardboard tubes, glass jars, and biodegradable wrapping. We're helping tackle the 120 billion plastic containers produced by the beauty industry each year.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Absolutely! You can pause, skip, or cancel your Subscribe & Save subscription anytime with no fees or commitments. Simply log into your account or email us at hello@naturallywyld.ca. We make it easy because we're confident you'll love our products.

Do you offer a money-back guarantee?

Yes! We offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you're not completely satisfied with any product, contact us within 30 days of purchase for a full refund. We stand behind the quality of our natural skincare products.

Are your products safe for sensitive skin?

Yes! Our products are specifically formulated for sensitive skin. We avoid common irritants like baking soda, synthetic fragrances, and harsh chemicals. Many customers with eczema, allergies, and sensitive skin use our products daily with great results.
